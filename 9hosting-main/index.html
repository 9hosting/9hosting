National Scholarship Portal (NSP) Scholarships

The following scholarships can be applied for only through the National Scholarship Portal (NSP). Follow the steps to register:

  1. Create an account on the NSP using this link.
  2. Complete the eKYC using your Aadhaar number.
  3. Install the NSP OTR app from the Play Store and complete face authentication.
  4. Receive your permanent OTR and apply on the NSP website.
